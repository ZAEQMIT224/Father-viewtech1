Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cost of a typical project?

Small interior upgrades may start around ₹3–10 lakhs, while full home builds can range from ₹40 lakhs to ₹3+ crores. We provide accurate estimates after an initial consultation. Varies if outside India.

Do you handle permits?

Yes, we manage all zoning and permitting for seamless execution.

How long does it take?

Design phase: 1-3 months; full build: 6-24 months, depending on scope.
